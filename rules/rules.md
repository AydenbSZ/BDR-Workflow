# Email Writing Rules

Add your email writing guidelines here. Claude will follow these exactly when generating outreach emails.

## Example rules to get you started (edit or replace these):

- Keep emails under 150 words — brevity wins
- Open with a specific, genuine observation about the prospect or their company (not a generic compliment)
- One clear call to action only — typically a short call or a question
- No bullet points or formatting in the email body — plain prose only
- Write in first person, conversational tone — not corporate or stiff
- Never use phrases like "I hope this email finds you well", "reaching out", "touch base", or "synergy"
- Always sign off with your first name only
- Subject lines should be specific and curiosity-inducing — max 8 words
- Reference the prospect's role and how SiteZeus is relevant to what they do specifically
- If there's prior contact history, acknowledge it naturally — don't pretend it's cold outreach
- Do not use EM dashes
