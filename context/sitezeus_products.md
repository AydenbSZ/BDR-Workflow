# SiteZeus — Company & Product Overview

## Company Overview

SiteZeus is an AI-powered location intelligence platform built for multi-unit and franchise brands. We help real estate, marketing, development, and franchise sales teams make faster, more confident decisions about where to grow, how to build, who to sell to, and how to market to customers.

**Core value proposition:** AI-driven insights that cost 1/5 to 1/10 the price of traditional site consultants — delivered in seconds instead of weeks.

**Who we serve:** Multi-unit restaurants, franchise systems, retail, convenience stores, grocery, healthcare, banking, and any brand with 10+ locations looking to grow intelligently.

**Background:** SiteZeus merged with Synuma in March 2023, combining site selection and location intelligence (SiteZeus) with franchise CRM and construction management (Synuma) to create a fully integrated growth platform.

---

## Product: Locate (Site Selection & Sales Forecasting)

**The flagship platform** — AI-powered site selection, sales forecasting, and portfolio optimization for real estate and development teams.

**Best for:** VP of Real Estate, Director of Development, Real Estate Managers, franchise owners evaluating new markets.

**Key capabilities:**
- **Zeustimate Sales Forecasting** — Real-time AI sales projections for any US location, generated in seconds (vs. weeks for traditional pin studies). Aggregates thousands of data points for extreme accuracy.
- **Site Sonar White Space Analysis** — Generates thousands of sales projections nationally in minutes with heat maps to identify top expansion markets.
- **Customer Segmentation Dashboard** — Behavioral segmentation using mobile geofencing (UberMedia) and geosocial data (Spatial.ai) — 80 distinct segment types based on actual behavior, not just demographics.
- **Territory Management** — Define and evaluate protected franchise territories across CBSAs, DMAs, and counties.
- **Variable Importance & Impact** — Transparency into exactly which factors drive model predictions and their dollar impact on performance.
- **Smart Comps** — Analytically finds the most comparable existing locations to a subject site.
- **Sandbox Portfolio Optimization** — Model remodel, relocation, and closure scenarios with full financial impact analysis.
- **Competitive Sales Comps** — View competitor revenues and co-tenant data.
- **Zeus.ai Chat** — Conversational AI assistant to ask questions about any site and get data-backed answers.
- **SitePak Wizard** — Rapid customizable reporting (5 templates) for site packages.

**The pain it solves:** Teams spending weeks on site packages, gut-feel decisions, expensive consultant fees, no visibility into which markets to prioritize.

## Product: Build (Construction & Development Management)

**Project management built specifically for multi-site development** — tracks every build from lease signing to grand opening.

**Best for:** VP of Development, Construction Manager, Director of Real Estate Operations, anyone managing multiple concurrent build projects.

**Key capabilities:**
- **Adaptive Timeline Tracking** — Customizable timelines that automatically shift future milestones when delays occur, keeping the whole schedule accurate in real time.
- **Centralized Project Dashboard** — All active development projects in one view with drill-down into individual sites.
- **Cost Analysis & Tracking** — Track planned vs. actual costs; build better estimates over time.
- **Pipeline Management** — Clear stage visibility from site identified → under construction → open.
- **Document Storage** — Centralized repository for all project documents, permits, plans, contracts.
- **Opening Checklists** — Automated pre-opening task lists to ensure nothing is missed before grand opening.
- **Task Assignment** — Assign tasks to team members with accountability tracking.
- **Integrated Communications** — Team collaboration and update sharing across all project stakeholders.

**The pain it solves:** Projects going over budget and schedule because timelines are managed in spreadsheets, documents scattered across emails, no single source of truth for development pipeline status.

## Product: Market (Customer Insights Platform)

**Also referred to as "Customer Insights"** — behavioral analytics platform that helps brands understand who their customers actually are and how to reach them.

**Best for:** VP of Marketing, CMO, Director of Digital Marketing, Marketing Analysts, operations teams trying to improve same-store sales.

**Key capabilities:**
- **Real-Time Behavioral Segmentation** — Up to 80 behavioral customer segments based on actual purchase behavior, online activity, and social media interests — not just age/income demographics.
- **Geofencing & Geosocial Data** — Powered by UberMedia mobile path-to-purchase data and Spatial.ai geosocial data.
- **Path-to-Purchase Tracking** — Understand the consumer journey from digital touchpoints to in-store visit.
- **Campaign Activation** — Export custom audiences directly to advertising platforms for highly targeted campaigns.
- **Ad Performance Analytics** — Measure campaign effectiveness and refine messaging.
- **Multi-Source Data Integration** — Append your own loyalty/transactional data with platform data for richer audience profiles.
- **Channel & Interest Insights** — Understand which channels customers prefer and what influences their decisions.
- **365-Day Historical Data** — Year of behavioral data for trend analysis.

**The pain it solves:** Brands guessing at who their customer is, spending ad budget on broad audiences, inability to connect marketing activity to in-store results.

## Product: Sell (Franchise Sales CRM)

**A CRM built specifically for franchise development** — manages the entire franchise sales pipeline from lead to signed franchisee.

**Best for:** VP of Franchise Development, Franchise Sales Director, Director of Franchise Growth, anyone managing franchise prospect pipelines.

**Key capabilities:**
- **Lead Qualification** — Customizable assessment tools to qualify prospects against your franchisee criteria.
- **Visual Pipeline Management** — Track every prospect from first inquiry through discovery day, validation, and FDD signing.
- **Outlook Integration** — Full email sync with Microsoft Outlook; all prospect communications tracked in one place.
- **FDD Signing Assistance** — Streamlines FDD workflow and tracks signing status.
- **Territory Assignment Management** — Align prospect locations with available territories; protect existing franchisees.
- **Document Storage & Correspondence** — Centralized contracts, correspondence, and compliance documents.
- **Franchisee Onboarding** — Automated workflows post-signing to transition new franchisees to operations.
- **Lead Source Technology** — Built-in lead sourcing to identify new potential franchisees.
- **ADA Compliance** — Built-in accessibility compliance features.

**The pain it solves:** Franchise sales teams working in generic CRMs not built for franchising, losing track of prospects, territory conflicts, FDD compliance gaps, slow onboarding.

## Competitive Differentiators

1. **Speed** — Zeustimate in seconds vs. weeks for pin studies; white space analysis for entire US in minutes.
2. **Cost** — 1/5 to 1/10 the cost of traditional site consultants.
3. **End-to-end platform** — Locate + Build + Market + Sell covers the full growth lifecycle; competitors offer point solutions.
4. **Transparency** — Synergy Power Score and Variable Importance show exactly how predictions are made and what drives them.
5. **Behavioral data** — Geosocial and geofencing data goes far beyond traditional demographics.
6. **Franchise-native** — Purpose-built for multi-unit brands; understands territory management, FDD compliance, franchisee dynamics.
